**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**:
- **Core Functionality**: Allow a marketing agency to manage clients and campaigns with financial tracking.
- **Tenancy Model**:
    - **Initial**: Single-tenant for one agency.
    - **Update (In Progress)**: Multi-tenant with a Super Admin role. The Super Admin can create and manage multiple organizations/agencies. They have a global dashboard view with a filter to switch between organizations. Other users (Admin, Team Member) are scoped to their specific organization.
- **Roles & Permissions**:
    - **Super Admin**: Manages all organizations.
    - **Admin**: Manages a single organization, including clients, campaigns, services, and team members. Has full financial visibility for their organization.
    - **Team Member**: Can only see assigned campaigns and deliverables. Can update the status of their deliverables. Has a Custom Access variant that restricts them to only inputting deliverables.
- **Financials**:
    - Currency: BDT (Bangladeshi Taka).
    - Define services with unit prices (e.g., Static Post = 1,600 BDT).
    - Campaigns have line items (Service x Quantity x Rate).
    - Track  (total value of all line items) vs.  (value of completed deliverables).
- **Deliverables Workflow**:
    - Statuses: Pending, In Progress, Review, Delivered/Live (managed via a dropdown).
    - Submitting proof (e.g., a URL) is optional to mark a deliverable as Delivered.
- **Team Management**: Admins can invite team members, assigning a role, and optionally a Designation and Department.
- **Audit Logs**: Track create, update, and delete actions for key entities (clients, campaigns, services, team members).
- **Date Filters**: The dashboard and other relevant views should have date filters (e.g., Today, This Week, This Month).
- **UI/UX**: Clean SaaS aesthetic using shadcn/ui and Tailwind CSS, with Recharts for data visualization.
